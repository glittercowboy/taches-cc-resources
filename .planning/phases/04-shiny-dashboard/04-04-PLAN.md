  <files>app/R/plot_functions.R</files>
  <action>
    Create `app/R/plot_functions.R`. Source this in `global.R` with `source(here::here("app","R","plot_functions.R"))`. Each function takes a filtered tibble and returns a plotly object.

    **Colour palette** (define once at top):
    ```r
    SENT_COLOURS <- c(positive="#2ca25f", negative="#de2d26", neutral="#636363", mixed="#fdae6b")
    ASPECT_COLOURS <- RColorBrewer::brewer.pal(8, "Set2")
    names(ASPECT_COLOURS) <- c("Accuracy & Reliability","Privacy & Data Security","Ethics & Bias",
                                "Regulation & Policy","Job Impact & Workflow","Patient Outcomes & Safety",
                                "Trust & Adoption","Cost & Accessibility")
    ```

    **1. `plot_aspect_frequency(df)`** — horizontal bar chart of aspect label frequency:
    - Filter to non-NA aspects; count by `aspect_label`, sort descending
    - ggplot2: `geom_col(fill = ASPECT_COLOURS[aspect_label])` + `coord_flip()`
    - Convert with `plotly::ggplotly(tooltip = c("x","y"))`
    - Title: "Most Discussed Aspects"

    **2. `plot_sentiment_by_aspect(df)`** — stacked 100% bar chart:
    - Cross-tab: aspect × aspect_sentiment (% of records per aspect)
    - ggplot2: `geom_bar(position = "fill", aes(fill = aspect_sentiment))` + `scale_fill_manual(SENT_COLOURS)` + `coord_flip()`
    - Convert to plotly
    - Title: "Sentiment Distribution by Aspect"

    **3. `plot_sentiment_over_time(df)`** — smoothed line chart:
    - Aggregate by `year_month`: compute % positive, % negative per month
    - ggplot2: `geom_line()` + `geom_smooth(se=FALSE, method="loess", span=0.4)` for both series
    - X axis: date (using `year_month` converted to Date)
    - Convert to plotly
    - Title: "Sentiment Trend Over Time"

    **4. `plot_emotion_distribution(df)`** — bar chart of top 10 emotion labels:
    - Count by `emotion_label` (record-level), top 10 by frequency
    - Colour by sentiment-adjacent grouping (joy/approval → green tones; anger/disgust/disapproval → red tones; sadness/fear → blue; neutral/surprise → grey)
    - ggplot2: `geom_col()` + `coord_flip()`
    - Convert to plotly
    - Title: "Emotion Distribution (Transformer)"

    **5. `plot_emotion_by_aspect(df)`** — heatmap (aspect × emotion):
    - Cross-tab: aspect_label × emotion_label, normalise within aspect (% of records per aspect)
    - Limit to top 8 emotions by overall frequency
    - ggplot2: `geom_tile(aes(fill = pct))` + `scale_fill_distiller(palette="YlOrRd", direction=1)`
    - Convert to plotly
    - Title: "Emotion Profile by Aspect"

    All functions handle empty/NA data gracefully — return a plotly with a "No data for current filters" message if `nrow(df) == 0` or all-NA relevant columns.
  </action>
